Our History

As part of the esteemed Babylon Group, founded in 1986, we share their entrepreneurial spirit and dedication to continuous growth. Babylon Resources Ltd. (BRL), established in 2014, is a leading Bangladeshi software development firm passionate about empowering businesses and individuals through innovative technology solutions.

Our Mission

Babylon Resources Limited has been carried forward the vision of building Digital Bangladesh and worked for fulfilling it since establishment. Our vision is to acquire ‘BRL Apps in Every Smartphone’

Our Vision

Our mission is to maintain strong relationship with customer to enable business improvement by streamlining business processes using advanced and latest technologies. We constantly lead through innovation to launch new products and services. We are different through our relentless attention to deliver excellence and zero customer service complaint.
